We are mHACKeroni, an Italian "ethical hacking" team.

Our "mega-team" is a joint collaboration between 5 Italian CTF teams, which merged together to sum their streghts and ultimately try to achieve DEFCON CTF Finals qualification:

  • Tower of Hanoi from Politecnico di Milano
  • c00kies@venice from the University of Venice Ca' Foscari
  • spritzers from the University of Padua
  • TheRomanXpl0it from Sapienza University of Rome
  • JBZ, an independent team

DEFCON 27 CTF Team

On Dec 27-29 2018, with the help of the KJC team, we placed first at the Chaos Communication Congress CTF over 640 teams from all over the world. This result gained us a pre-qualification to the DEF CON 27 CTF finals always in August in Las Vegas, USA. On August 9 to 11, 2019, we played the DEF CON CTF finals and achieved the 5th place and the first place among the european teams.

DEFCON 26 CTF Team

On May 12-13 2018, in a tremendous team effort, we scored second place on the DEF CON Capture The Flag qualifiers over 600 teams from all over the world. This result gained us access to the DEF CON 26 CTF finals in August in Las Vegas, USA. On August 10 to 12, 2018, we played the DEF CON CTF finals and achieved the 7th place on the leaderboard of one of the most important hacking competitions in the world!
