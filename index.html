Italian CTF team. DEF CON finalists. Cyber. Pasta. Hack-A-Sat 4 Winners.


What is mhackeroni?
#

mhackeroni is an Italian CTF team, founded in 2018 by a group of friends and security enthusiasts from various academic institutions who merged togheter to sum their streghts and ultimately achieve DEFCON CTF Finals qualification.

Our proud moments
#

We have consistently qualified for the DEFCON CTF Finals since 2018, with only a brief interruption along the way. After competing in several editions of Hack-A-Sat, we proudly emerged victorious in the 2023 competition.
